## 拉马尔相关著作 Related Works by Lamarre

Lamarre, T. (2002). From animation to anime: Drawing movements and moving drawings. *Japan Forum*, 14(2), 329–367.  
→ 早期论文，为《动画机器》的前期研究。探讨了"绘"与"动"的关系。

Lamarre, T. (2006). The multiplanar image. *Mechademia*, 1, 120–143.  
→ 《动画机器》第一章的早期版本。

Lamarre, T. (2008). Species of spaces. *Mechademia*, 3, 77–91.  
→ 多平面空间类型的进一步分类。

Lamarre, T. (2018). *The Anime Ecology: A Genealogy of Television, Animation, and Game Media*. Minneapolis, MN: University of Minnesota Press.  
→ 后续著作，将分析从"机器"扩展到"生态"。

## 理论来源 Theoretical Sources

### 德勒兹-加塔利 (机器概念)
Deleuze, G., & Guattari, F. (1980). *Mille plateaux: Capitalisme et schizophrénie*. Paris: Minuit.  
英译: *A Thousand Plateaus: Capitalism and Schizophrenia* (B. Massumi, Trans.). Minneapolis: University of Minnesota Press, 1987.  
→ 拉马尔借用的核心概念：machine（机器/装配）、deterritorialization（解域化）、assemblage（装配体）。

Deleuze, G. (1983). *Cinéma 1: L'Image-mouvement*. Paris: Minuit.  
英译: *Cinema 1: The Movement-Image* (H. Tomlinson & B. Habberjam, Trans.). Minneapolis: University of Minnesota Press, 1986.

Deleuze, G. (1985). *Cinéma 2: L'Image-temps*. Paris: Minuit.  
英译: *Cinema 2: The Time-Image* (H. Tomlinson & R. Galeta, Trans.). Minneapolis: University of Minnesota Press, 1989.  
→ 拉马尔与德勒兹电影理论的对话：德勒兹的"运动-影像"建立在电影基础上，拉马尔问：如果是动画呢？

### 朗西埃 (可感物的分配)
Rancière, J. (2000). *Le partage du sensible: Esthétique et politique*. Paris: La Fabrique.  
英译: *The Politics of Aesthetics: The Distribution of the Sensible* (G. Rockhill, Trans.). London: Continuum, 2004.  
→ 拉马尔借用的核心概念：distribution of the sensible（可感物的分配/感性的分割）。

### 麦克拉伦 (动画间隔)
McLaren, N. (n.d.). "Animation is not the art of drawings that move but the art of movements that are drawn."  
→ 虽非严格学术引文，但拉马尔将麦克拉伦的动画哲学作为"间隔"概念的重要来源。参见McLaren在NFB的访谈与讲稿。

### 媒介考古学
Kittler, F. (1986). *Grammophon, Film, Typewriter*. Berlin: Brinkmann & Bose.  
英译: *Gramophone, Film, Typewriter* (G. Winthrop-Young & M. Wutz, Trans.). Stanford: Stanford University Press, 1999.

Zielinski, S. (2002). *Archäologie der Medien*. Reinbek: Rowohlt.  
英译: *Deep Time of the Media: Toward an Archaeology of Hearing and Seeing by Technical Means* (G. Custance, Trans.). Cambridge, MA: MIT Press, 2006.

## 日本动画研究相关 Related Anime Studies

Clements, J., & McCarthy, H. (2006). *The Anime Encyclopedia: A Guide to Japanese Animation Since 1917* (Rev. ed.). Berkeley, CA: Stone Bridge Press.

Condry, I. (2013). *The Soul of Anime: Collaborative Creativity and Japan's Media Success Story*. Durham, NC: Duke University Press.  
→ 对"有限动画"的经济-社会成因提供了与拉马尔相反的视角（强调产业协作而非技术本体论）。

Napier, S. J. (2005). *Anime from Akira to Howl's Moving Castle: Experiencing Contemporary Japanese Animation* (Updated ed.). New York: Palgrave Macmillan.

Steinberg, M. (2012). *Anime's Media Mix: Franchising Toys and Characters in Japan*. Minneapolis, MN: University of Minnesota Press.  
→ 同出版社，讨论了动画的跨媒介生态，可视为拉马尔"机器"的外延。

Ōtsuka, E. (2010). World and variation: The reproduction and consumption of narrative. (M. Steinberg, Trans.). *Mechademia*, 5, 99–116.  
→ 大塚英志的"世界与变奏"理论，对理解有限动画的素材库系统（bank system）有补充价值。

Crafton, D. (1993). *Before Mickey: The Animated Film 1898–1928*. Chicago: University of Chicago Press.

Maltin, L. (1987). *Of Mice and Magic: A History of American Animated Cartoons* (Rev. ed.). New York: Plume.
